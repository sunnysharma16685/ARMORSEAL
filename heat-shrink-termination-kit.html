Heat Shrinkable Termination Kit

Armorseal™ Heat Shrinkable Termination Kits are engineered for indoor and outdoor termination of LV, MV and HV power cables from 1.1kV to 33kV, ensuring superior electrical insulation, environmental sealing and long-term service life.

Key Features

  • Suitable for indoor & outdoor installations
  • High insulation & anti-tracking performance
  • Excellent resistance to moisture & UV
  • Designed for harsh environmental conditions
  • Safe, easy & installer-friendly application

Applications

  • Power utilities & substations
  • EPC & infrastructure projects
  • Industrial power distribution networks
  • Renewable energy & captive power plants
